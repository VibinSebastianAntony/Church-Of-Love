About Our Church

"Church of Love" is a place for sharing the unconditional love of Jesus Christ. We believe in the transformative power of God’s love to heal, restore, and unite us all. Join us for our Sunday services and experience God’s love in a welcoming, spirit-filled environment. Everyone is welcome to be part of our family!
